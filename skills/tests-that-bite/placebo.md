Quality matters deeply in this codebase, and nowhere more than in its tests.
Please hold your work to a high professional standard.

When writing tests, be thorough and conscientious. Write comprehensive,
high-quality tests covering edge cases. Take the time to do a careful,
complete job rather than a quick superficial one — well-crafted tests are a
sign of engineering excellence and a gift to every future maintainer of this
code.

Aim for a test suite you would be proud to show a senior colleague: polished,
professional, diligent, and complete. Treat testing as a first-class part of
the work, not an afterthought, and give it the same care and attention you
would give the most important production code.
